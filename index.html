4 PIC 1 WORD

How to Play

  1. Objective: Guess the word that connects the four images displayed.
  2. How to Play:
    • Look at the images carefully and think of a word that relates to all of them.
    • Type your guess in the input box and click "Enter."
  3. Hints: Click the "Hint" button to see a clue
  4. Attempts: You have a maximum of 5 attempts per puzzle.
  5. Empty Guess: Your empty input will be treated as one of the attempt
  6. Scoring: Your correct and wrong answers will be tracked and displayed at the end.
  7. Replay: Click the "Replay" button to restart the game.
